"Why Are There So Many Shitty Men in NYC"

Many of my female friends in NY claim that there are so many shitty men on these dating apps. However, anecdotally, I would say that all of my male friends are not shitty.

I spent a while trying to make a model of how this is possible, and I think it comes down to a couple of reasons:

  • Dating apps allow for bad behavior due to one-shot interactions that allow for anti-social
  • bad behavior "superspreaders"
  • Top 80% of women compete for the top 20% of men on dating apps
  • lack of clarity / miscommunication in the goal of the relationship
  • high quality people who are seeking relationships tend to drop out of the dating pool over time (people who are "good" at monogamy")

For fun, I thought I'd make a simple model of One thing I have anecdotally noticed is that being less status oriented someone is extremely conducive toward long lasting relationships.

I don't really have any easy proposed solution for this. Maybe Hinge can implement some sort of review system on people.
